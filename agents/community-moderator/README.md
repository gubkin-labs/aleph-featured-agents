# Community Moderator Lite

**Who it’s for:** Creators and community owners who moderate Discord or Telegram without a full mod-bot stack.

**What it does on a schedule:** Once a day it posts a short moderation digest from remembered norms and recurring patterns, and asks whether rules need updates.

**What to set after clone:**

1. Enable the agent.
2. Paste your server rules and preferred reply voice in chat (stored in memory).
3. Connect Discord (recommended) or Telegram under **Channels**.
4. In chat, paste messy threads or incidents for summary + draft replies.

**Channel tip:** Best with Discord connected so you can forward or discuss cases where your community already lives.

No vault secrets required for the agent itself (your bot token lives in Aleph Channels / vault when you connect).
