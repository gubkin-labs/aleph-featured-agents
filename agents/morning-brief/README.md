# Morning Brief

**Who it’s for:** Individuals who want a proactive daily digest without opening a news app.

**What it does on a schedule:** Every morning (08:00 UTC by default) it pulls weather for your saved city, searches a few headlines on your topics, and posts a short focus note. If Discord or Telegram is connected, the brief can deliver there.

**What to set after clone:**

1. Enable the agent.
2. Chat once: tell it your city, news topics, and preferred brief length — it stores these in memory.
3. Optionally edit `schedules.toml` timezone or cron after cloning if you want a different local morning.

**Channel tip:** Connect Discord or Telegram under **Channels** so the scheduled brief reaches you where you already chat.

No vault secrets required.
