# Habit Coach

**Who it’s for:** Individuals who want light accountability without a full habit-tracking app.

**What it does on a schedule:** Morning and evening UTC check-ins ask whether you did today’s habits and update streaks in memory. Chat anytime to log a win, skip a day, or change goals.

**What to set after clone:**

1. Enable the agent.
2. Tell it 1–3 habits and your preferred nudge style (gentle or direct).
3. Optionally adjust cron/timezone in `schedules.toml` after cloning.

**Channel tip:** Connect Discord or Telegram under **Channels** so check-ins land in your chat.

No vault secrets required.
