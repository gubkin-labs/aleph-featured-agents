# Support Triage

**Who it’s for:** Creators and small businesses that answer support in chat or email without a full helpdesk.

**What it does on a schedule:** Once a day it reviews remembered open items and suggests unresolved themes plus FAQ candidates.

**What to set after clone:**

1. Enable the agent.
2. Paste product facts, FAQ snippets, and brand voice in chat (stored in memory).
3. Paste customer messages anytime for category, priority, and a draft reply.

**Channel tip:** Connect Discord or Telegram under **Channels** if your support already lives there; otherwise use Aleph chat.

No vault secrets required.
